DESI ANWAR

Desi Anwar is Senior Anchor, TV Journalist and Producer, Writer, Photographer, Media & Communications Specialist based in Jakarta, Indonesia. Currently she is the host of 'Face 2 Face with Desi Anwar,' on Metro TV, a programme where she interviews prominent figures and personalities around the world. Her guests include: HH the Dalai Lama, Entrepreneur Richard Branson, Hollywood star Richard Gere, Musician Kitaro, Financier George Soros and many heads of states and other influential international figures.
